Air Freight

Fast. When timing is everything.

Air freight is the right call when your show is close, your freight is time-sensitive, or you simply can't afford the risk of a longer transit. We coordinate with airlines and freight handlers globally to get your materials where they need to be.

Express and standard air options — matched to your show dates and budget
Customs clearance at origin and destination, managed end-to-end
Sensitive and high-value cargo handled with appropriate care and documentation
Inbound to Australia or outbound — we work both directions
Sea Freight

Cost-effective for larger loads.

When you have more time and more volume, sea freight is often the smarter choice. Full container or shared — we coordinate the booking, documentation, and delivery at both ends through our international agent network.

FCL (Full Container Load) for larger or dedicated shipments
LCL (Less Than Container Load) when you don't need the whole box
Ro/Ro and breakbulk for oversized, wheeled, or non-standard cargo
Full documentation — bills of lading, packing lists, customs paperwork handled
What we manage

The paperwork, the compliance,
the coordination

International freight involves a lot of moving parts beyond the transport itself. Here's what we take off your plate.

Customs clearance

Import and export customs at both ends — declarations, duties, compliance with local regulations. We handle this as standard, not as an add-on.

ATA Carnets

Temporary importation for goods that will be returned after the show — display items, equipment, samples. We coordinate carnet documentation and ensure everything is returned correctly.

02
Shipping documentation

Bills of lading, airway bills, commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin — all prepared and verified before freight moves.

03
International agent network

We work with trusted freight agents in key markets worldwide. They handle the local side — we handle your side. One point of contact for the whole journey.

04
Freight insurance

General coverage included. Additional insurance available for high-value, fragile, or sensitive items — declare at the time of booking and we'll arrange it.

05
Return freight

We coordinate the outbound and the return. After the show, freight is collected and shipped back — or held if needed for the next event.

How we work

Event freight specialists, not general forwarders

There's a meaningful difference between a general freight forwarder and someone who specialises in events and exhibitions.

General forwarders move boxes. We move exhibition freight — which means we understand tight bump-in windows, venue receiving requirements, temporary importation, and what it means when something goes wrong the day before a show opens.

We've built our international operation around event timelines, not standard transit schedules. And because we handle the domestic side too, we can manage the full journey — from overseas origin to your stand — without handing off to someone who doesn't know the show.
