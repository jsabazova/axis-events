What's included

No two shows are the same. We don't treat them that way.

Whether you need a basic forklift crew for bump-in or a full show management operation — we handle everything that happens behind the scenes. Dock management, crew, equipment, storage, the lot. Tell us about your show and we'll put the right operation together around it.

Expertise & Advisory
We've run logistics on events of all sizes. Before your show, we'll advise on the equipment and crew you actually need — based on your freight volume, venue, and schedule. So you're not over or under-resourced on the day.
Equipment
Forklifts, cranes, elevated work platforms — we source and sort everything required. If specialist attachments or additional equipment are needed during the show, we'll get them to site. Pallet jacks and trolleys are available where included or required.
On-Site Crew
Experienced forklift operators, spotters, site managers, supervisors, dock managers, porters, and general labour. We scale the team to your event. A basic crew handles deliveries — a full team manages the whole operation.
Dock & Delivery Management
We run a dock management system — truck slots are pre-booked to control traffic flow, reducing congestion and wait times during bump-in and bump-out. We run the dock so it doesn't become your problem.
Storage
From suitcases and small goods through to pallets and large crates — we handle pickup from and delivery back to stands. If space is tight, we can offer storage solutions to handle any surplus.
Safety & Compliance
All work is performed legally, safely, and to venue requirements. We handle all required inductions, certifications, and compliance — so nothing gets held up on the day.
Supplies
Things get forgotten or run out — pallet wrap, tape, markers, vests and everything else you might need on the day is always available on-site for purchase.

How we work with you

Two ways to engage us.
Every show is different.

Organiser-Managed

You're in control.

You engage Axis and pay for the crew and equipment you need. Works well for smaller shows — sometimes just a forklift operator and a spotter. It scales for larger shows too, though as complexity grows, so does the investment. We put together the right team based on your show size and predicted freight volume.

  • Pay for exactly what you need — nothing more
  • Tailored to your show size and freight volume
  • Clear pricing upfront — no surprises after the event
Exhibitor-Funded

Full service. No cost to you.

The organiser gets a fully managed on-site logistics service at no cost. The service is funded by charging exhibitors directly for forklifting, storage, porterage, and any labour they require. Extras are added or removed based on what's needed to keep operations running efficiently. A minimum freight volume needs to be met — not sure if your show qualifies? We'll work it out with you.

  • Zero cost to the organiser
  • Exhibitors charged only for what they actually use
  • Includes service desk, dock management & real-time freight notifications
  • Minimum freight volume applies — ask us if you qualify
