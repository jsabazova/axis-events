1. Overview

Axis Events Pty Ltd (ABN: [INSERT ABN]) is committed to protecting your privacy. We are bound by the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, hold, and disclose your personal information. By using our website or engaging our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. What We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, company name, and job title
  • Contact details including email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Freight and shipment details (dimensions, weight, contents, origin, destination)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party payment systems — we do not store card details)
  • Event and exhibitor details relevant to your booking
  • Correspondence and communications with us
  • Website usage data (via cookies and analytics — see Section 7)

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for our services or as required by law.

3. How We Collect It

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you when you submit a quote request, make a booking, or contact us
  • From event organisers or venue operators who share exhibitor details with us for logistical purposes
  • Through our website via contact forms or quote tools
  • Automatically via cookies and website analytics tools when you visit our site

Where reasonable, we will collect information directly from you rather than from third parties.

4. How We Use It

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and manage freight and logistics services
  • Process bookings, quotes, and invoices
  • Communicate with you about your booking, freight status, and event logistics
  • Coordinate with carriers, venues, and event organisers on your behalf
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Send relevant service updates or announcements (you can opt out at any time)

We will not use your personal information for purposes beyond what is described here without your consent.

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Carriers and transport providers — to fulfil freight and delivery services
  • Event organisers and venues — where necessary to coordinate logistics access and operations
  • Customs and government bodies — where required for international freight compliance
  • IT and software service providers — who assist in running our systems and platforms, under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal or regulatory authorities — where required by law

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Where we disclose information to overseas parties (e.g. for international freight), we take reasonable steps to ensure your information is handled consistently with Australian privacy standards.

6. Storage & Security

We store personal information securely, using industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. This includes:

  • Secure, password-protected systems with access controls
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) on our website
  • Regular reviews of our data handling practices

We retain personal information for as long as is necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations, after which it is securely deleted or de-identified.

While we take reasonable precautions, no data transmission or storage system is 100% secure. If you have concerns about a potential data breach, please contact us immediately.

7. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is aggregated and anonymised and is used solely to improve the website experience.

Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect some website functionality.

We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information without your knowledge.

8. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request that we delete your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe your privacy rights have been breached

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to requests within a reasonable timeframe.
